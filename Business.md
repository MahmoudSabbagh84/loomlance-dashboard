# LoomLance — Business Overview

> The all-in-one workspace for independent freelancers. One hub for clients, projects, contracts, and invoices — so you can spend your time on the work, not on wrangling tools.

---

## Introduction

Freelancing today means running a real business with the tooling of a hobby. A typical independent professional juggles a project board in one app, an invoicing tool in another, contracts in a third, client notes in a spreadsheet, and the actual money story scattered across email threads and bank statements. Every tool charges its own subscription, none of them talk to each other, and the freelancer becomes the integration layer — copying numbers, chasing payments, and losing hours to admin every week.

**LoomLance weaves all of it into a single, clean workspace.** Clients, projects with kanban boards, contracts, and invoices live in one place, share one source of truth, and roll up into one dashboard that tells you — at a glance — what you're owed, what's due, and what to do next. It's the operational backbone for a freelance business, built for the way independent professionals actually work.

LoomLance is delivered as a hosted SaaS product on a subscription model, with three tiers that scale from a free starting point to a full professional toolkit.

## The Product

Every LoomLance account — including the free tier — includes the core platform:

- **Unified Dashboard** — revenue this month, outstanding balances, open invoices, and active projects at a glance, plus a "due soon" panel, recent activity feed, and a guided onboarding checklist for new accounts.
- **Client Hub** — a directory of clients with multiple contacts each, a primary-contact designation, and a per-client view that ties together their projects, contracts, and invoices.
- **Projects & Kanban Boards** — every project gets a drag-and-drop kanban board with customizable columns, work-in-progress limits, task priorities, labels, and due dates. Built to run real delivery, not just track to-dos.
- **Contracts** — create and manage client agreements, attach signed PDF documents in secure storage, and track each contract's status and value.
- **Invoicing** — professional invoices with line items, taxes and discounts, live totals, and a full status lifecycle (draft → sent → viewed → paid, plus overdue and void). Per-business sequential invoice numbering and recorded payments keep your books clean.
- **Profile & Business Settings** — your display name, business details (name, tax ID, address), default currency, and subscription management in one place.
- **Polished, themeable interface** — a refined dark-first design with a light mode, multi-currency support, and in-app notifications.

What changes between tiers is **how many active projects you can run** and **which advanced, leverage-creating features you unlock**.

## The Three Tiers

LoomLance is offered in three subscription tiers. The free tier is a complete, genuinely useful product on its own; the paid tiers add capacity and professional-grade automation, branding, and insight.

### 🆓 Free — *Get started*

For freelancers trying LoomLance or running a single engagement at a time.

- The complete core platform: dashboard, clients, projects & kanban, contracts, and invoicing
- **1 active project** at a time
- Standard (unbranded) invoices
- Dark/light themes, multi-currency, notifications

**Best for:** newcomers, side projects, and anyone who wants to evaluate the full workflow before upgrading.

---

### ⭐ Tier 1 — *Professional*

For working freelancers running several clients and projects who want to look polished and automate routine billing.

Everything in Free, plus:

- **Up to 5 active projects**
- **Custom invoice branding** — add your logo, accent color, and a custom footer to every invoice
- **Recurring invoices** — set up invoices that send themselves on a schedule, automating monthly retainers and subscriptions
- **Time tracking** — a built-in timer and manual time entries, with the ability to generate invoices directly from tracked billable hours

**Best for:** established freelancers with a steady client base who bill regularly and want their brand front-and-center.

---

### 🚀 Tier 2 — *Business*

For full-time independents and micro-studios running their entire business on LoomLance and wanting complete financial visibility.

Everything in Tier 1, plus:

- **Unlimited active projects**
- **Expense tracking** — log expenses by category and project and upload receipts, so every job's true profitability is captured
- **Reports** — detailed reporting on revenue, profit & loss, invoice aging, and tracked time, turning your day-to-day activity into business intelligence

**Best for:** full-time freelancers and small studios who treat LoomLance as their operational and financial backbone.

## How Subscriptions Work

LoomLance is split across two connected products that share a single account and sign-in:

- **Loomlance Splash** — the public site that handles signup, pricing, and subscription checkout via **Stripe**. This is where a freelancer creates an account, chooses a tier, and manages billing.
- **LoomLance Dashboard** — the post-login application (this product) where the actual work happens: clients, projects, contracts, and invoices.

Both share one secure backend, so a freelancer signs up once and their subscription tier flows straight through to the features they can access in the Dashboard. Plan changes, upgrades, and billing are always managed on the main site; the Dashboard reflects the active plan and gently surfaces upgrade paths when a freelancer reaches a limit or taps a premium feature.

Each tier corresponds to a Stripe subscription, and a freelancer can move up or down at any time — their data and history are preserved across changes.
